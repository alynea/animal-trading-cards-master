  • Origin: Greek Mythology
  • Etymology: Luwian meaning "lightning"
  • Other names: Unicorn, Qilin, Buraq

A pure white, winged stallion, Pegasus is often found aiding the greatest heros in their feats. He is associated with wisdom and eternal fame. Zeus eventually placed him among the constellations. The Great square of Pegasus constellation depicts the horse's body.
